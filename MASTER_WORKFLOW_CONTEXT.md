# MASTER WORKFLOW CONTEXT

## The Seed
- **Animal:** Sloth
- **Direction / Emotion:** Exhausted / Weary

## The Cultural Vibe
On the internet (TikTok, Reddit, Gen Z/Millennial humor), the sloth is no longer just "lazy." It is the ultimate mascot for the "soft life," "quiet quitting," and "anti-hustle" culture. It represents chaotic exhaustion, being "chronically cozy," and "emotionally buffering." The vibe is a self-aware, gentle irony where doing absolutely nothing or moving with zero urgency is celebrated as a valid response to burnout culture. Key phrases include "running on caffeine & denial," "doing the bare minimum and loving it," and "my brain is not braining today." It's not about being a loser; it's about unapologetic, aesthetic resting.

## The Format Route
**T-Shirt / Crewneck Apparel**
(While stickers work for bite-sized memes, the "exhausted sloth" translates incredibly well to parody "Athletic Club" apparel, retro graphics, and bold "Anti-Hustle" statements worn as a badge of honor on a cozy, oversized tee or sweater).

## Raw Concept Angles
1. **The "Anti-Hustle" Parody Club:** A vintage-style "Already Tired Athletic Club" or "No-Hustle Track & Field" graphic, featuring a sloth as the unmotivated mascot. Contrasting sporty, varsity aesthetics with pure exhaustion.
2. **Tech-Fatigue / "Emotionally Buffering":** A spaced-out sloth with a retro Windows loading hourglass, a "1% battery" indicator, or text reading "Offline Until Further Notice." Perfectly captures the "brain not braining" vibe.
3. **Chronically Cozy / Soft Life Champion:** A sloth completely bundled in an oversized blanket with an iced coffee or mug, looking unapologetically tired. Text like "Surviving via Naps" or "Resting is my Passion."
4. **The Dramatic Introvert:** A minimalist, deadpan sloth graphic with small, highly relatable text like "Moving slowly out of respect for the vibe" or "Too tired to panic."

- **Typography:** Bold vintage block letters
- **Environment:** Single prop interaction (vintage folding chair)

- **Phrase under 8 words?** Yes (3 words).

- **Parody elements?** Yes (mimics varsity/athletic club apparel).
- **Shows, doesn't tell?** Yes (the sloth melting over a chair implies the missing event/activity).

### 🖼️ MASTER COMPOSITION PROMPT
**[The Medium & Format]**
A flat screenprint-style t-shirt graphic of a sloth, designed as an arched text over mascot composition.

**[The Subject & Emotional Paradox]**
A full-body sloth with half-open deadpan eyes and a completely boneless, melting physicality, conveying a sense of chaotic self-acceptance of total exhaustion.

**[The Physical Weight & Placement]**
The mascot is draped limply like a heavy, wet towel over the back of a vintage metal folding chair, arms dangling entirely relaxed toward the ground, fully surrendering its weight to gravity, interacting exclusively with this single prop.

**[The Typography & Compositional Intent]**
The design features the phrase "ACTIVELY NOT PARTICIPATING" in bold vintage block letters. Text is flat, legible, and not warped onto 3D surfaces, positioned arched beautifully over the top of the melting sloth. This creates a humorous imbalance between the demanding, collegiate-style text and the totally unbothered, collapsed character.

**[The Rendering Shield]**
Color palette: rustic earth tones (cream, burnt sienna, rustic green, mustard). Flat colors only, no gradients, no shading, no glossy rendering. Bold simplified shapes, clean outlines, strong silhouette readability, restrained linework with vintage engraving influence. Transparent background. Minimal decorative elements only (single thematic prop). NO full scenic backgrounds.

**[The Negative Constraints]**
No mockup, no shirt shown, isolated graphic only. Portrait orientation, optimized for print. Avoid photorealism, 3D rendering, anime style, watercolor, cinematic lighting, cluttered composition, multiple competing focal points, extra limbs, malformed anatomy, AI artifacting, gibberish text, decorative fake lettering, smooth gradients, glossy rendering, modern digital fonts, detailed scenery.
