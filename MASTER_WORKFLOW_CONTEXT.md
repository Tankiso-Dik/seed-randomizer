## Phase 1: Context Brief

**The Seed**
- **Animal:** Pig

**The Cultural Vibe**
The internet's perception of "pigs" in a wholesome/playful context splits into two lanes:

1. **Fade Pig / Buzz Cut Pig (Absurd Confidence):** Originating from AI-generated images posted by Facebook user Arctic on Sept 12, 2025, the "Fade Pig" meme features a piglet with a buzzed haircut on a conveyor belt. It went massively viral on X (54K+ likes) and TikTok throughout late 2025 and into 2026, used to represent an "ugly boyfriend" or someone with a bad haircut who is completely unbothered by it. The humor is pure absurdist confidence — a pig that thinks it looks cool but is objectively ridiculous. Still actively trending in mid-2026.

2. **Wholesome Pig Core (PigTok / Tutu-Core):** A 2026 wave of high-fidelity pink pig characters set to pop music ("tutu-core"), cute piglets in Easter outfits, and "Pig Core" aesthetic on TikTok. This lane is feel-good, soft, and playful but heavily saturated on Etsy/Redbubble with generic "cute pig" and "pig lover" designs.

**The Format Route**
**Format:** T-Shirt
**Reasoning:** The Fade Pig meme is a hot cultural moment (mid-2026) and works best as a bold, confident tee design. The "ugly but confident" angle has strong ironic streetwear potential. The generic cute pig market is oversaturated on Etsy/Redbubble, so leaning into the absurdist Fade Pig lane differentiates from floral "pig mom" designs.

**Market Intent Confidence Score:** High — Fade Pig is actively trending across TikTok, X, Instagram, and YouTube as of mid-2026. The meme has proven multi-platform virality and strong cultural recognition.

**Raw Concept Angles**
1. **"Confidently Mid" (Fade Pig / Absurd Confidence):** A pig with a terrible buzz cut, striking a smug, self-satisfied pose. The phrase plays on the Fade Pig's energy of being ugly but thinking they're hot. Vintage athletic mascot style.
2. **"Fresh Cut. Same Pig." (Self-Aware Fade):** A pig with a fresh fade, looking in a mirror with pure delight. The phrase signals knowing you look stupid but owning it completely.
3. **"Pig Core" (Tutu-Core Crossover):** A chunky pink pig in a tiny tutu, striking a ballet pose with deadpan serious expression. Merges the tutu-core trend with deadpan humor.
4. **"Unbothered. Moisturized. In My Lane." (Fade Pig Affirmations):** A pig with a buzz cut striking a model pose. Taps into the "confidence delusion" register while keeping it wholesome.
