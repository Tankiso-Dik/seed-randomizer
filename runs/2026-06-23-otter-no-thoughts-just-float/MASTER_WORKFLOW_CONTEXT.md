# 🎨 Phase 2: Prompt Maker Output

## Unified Joke Statement
> *"The joke is: an otter floating belly-up with empty, half-closed eyes and a tiny blissed-out smile, completely at peace with having absolutely nothing going on upstairs — the viewer laughs because they recognize their own overstimulated brain's fantasy of pure mental emptiness. The otter's serene posture contradicts the absurdity of a highly intelligent animal claiming to have zero thoughts, and the viewer sees themselves in that contradiction."*

---

## 🪝 Identity Hook (The "Me Too" Check)

**1. The Human Feeling:**
The overwhelming desire to turn your brain off and just *exist* — to escape the constant pressure to think, plan, overanalyze, and perform. Pure empty-headed bliss as a coping mechanism for an overstimulated, information-saturated world.

**2. The "Why Wear It" (Identity Signal):**
"I'm mentally exhausted but I've made peace with it. I'm not broken — I'm just floating through life. This is my badge of surrender, my membership card in the burnout/brainrot community." The wearer signals they're in on the joke: they know modern life is too much, and they've chosen the otter's path of serene emptiness.

**3. The Punchline:**
The otter's natural floating posture (peaceful, iconic, beloved) paired with the confession of having zero brain activity — which is hilarious because otters are famously intelligent tool-users. The contradiction between the serene visual and the "no thoughts" text IS the joke. You laugh because you *wish* you could be this empty.

## 🎬 Director's Brain — Style Choices

| Dimension | Selection | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Format** | **Format A (Suspicious Close-Up)** | Tight crop to head + upper body/shoulders. Otter floating on its back. Text placed BELOW. Maximizes emotional impact of the expression on a vertical 3:4 t-shirt canvas. Avoids the horizontal-composition-on-vertical-canvas problem of a full-body floating otter. |
| **Emotional Paradox** | **Serene delivery of chaotic content** | Calm otter, confessional/unhinged phrase. The tension between the peaceful face and the "no thoughts" text is the joke engine. |
| **Expression** | **Tired Cluster** | Thousand-yard stare, eyes half-closed mid-blink, one eye slightly more closed than the other. Tiny neutral/contented mouth curve. Head tilted slightly back (floating position). This micro-expression says "I'm here but I'm not here" — perfect for the joke. |
| **Hero Prop** | **NONE** | The floating-on-back posture IS the joke. Adding a prop (sunglasses, hat, etc.) would distract from the "pure emptiness" of the expression and violate the Cohesion Guardrail. The paws resting limp on the belly communicate everything. |

| **Typography** | **Bold retro collegiate block** (varsity/athletic) | Cream letters with thick charcoal black outline. Two-line centered layout below the graphic. Plain flat 2D lettering — no 3D text, no drop shadows, no extrusion. |

## 🖼️ Master Composition Prompt

### [THE MEDIUM & FORMAT]
> *"A flat screenprint-style t-shirt graphic on a transparent background of an otter, designed as a Format A Suspicious Close-Up — a tight crop showing only the head, shoulders, and upper body of the otter floating on its back."*

### [THE SUBJECT & EMOTIONAL PARADOX]
> *"An otter floating on its back, head tilted back at a 15-degree angle, with a thousand-yard stare: eyes half-closed mid-blink, one eye slightly more closed than the other. The mouth forms a tiny neutral-to-slightly-contented curve — not quite a smile, not quite a frown. Two front paws rest limply on the belly, completely relaxed. The expression and posture convey serene emptiness — totally at peace, not a single thought behind the eyes. A sense of calm delivery of absurd confessional content."*

### [PHYSICAL WEIGHT, STATIC GEOMETRY & NATURAL ASYMMETRY]
> *"The mascot is frozen in a floating-on-back pose, head tilted back approximately 15 degrees. Weight is distributed evenly as if floating on water. Natural asymmetry: one ear sits slightly higher than the other, one eye is slightly more closed, shoulders are not perfectly level — one slopes down slightly. Exactly two front paws are visible, resting limp on the belly/chest area, clearly separated from each other and the torso. Simple, chunky cartoon paws with NO individual fingers or toes. The head is slightly larger than realistic proportions (70% animal, 30% stylization). Short thick tail curves off below the frame, not visible in the tight crop. No extra limbs. Simplified fur shapes — clean, bold silhouettes with no individual fur strands."*

### [TYPOGRAPHY, TEXT ISOLATION & SPELLING SHIELD]
> *"The text phrase is laid out in two centered lines: 'NO THOUGHTS.' on line 1, 'JUST FLOAT.' on line 2. The letters are set in a bold retro collegiate varsity block font — thick, chunky, athletic letterforms. Letters are solid cream/off-white with a thick uniform charcoal black outline. Text is positioned BELOW the otter graphic, centered horizontally. A clean negative space gap of approximately 15% of the canvas height separates the bottom of the otter from the top of the text. The text does not wrap around, overlap, or touch the animal. Plain flat 2D lettering only — no 3D text, no 3D extrusion, no drop shadows on text, no spelling mistakes, no decorative flourishes."*

### [RENDERING SHIELD & COLOR COHESION]
> *"Color palette: Charcoal black (#2C2C2C) for outlines, main text, and eye detail. Rust/terracotta orange (#C45A3C) for the otter's body fur fill. Cream/off-white (#F5F0E8) used as negative space/paper showing through for the belly, muzzle area, paw undersides, and text. Flat colors only, bold color blocking, no gradients. The cream text color matches the cream belly of the otter for visual harmony. Grounded simplified mascot anatomy (70% animal, 30% stylization). Thick, confident uniform charcoal black outlines (approximately 3-4px equivalent stroke weight). Stipple/halftone shading texture applied to the rust orange body fill to simulate vintage screen print aging. Visible screen print ink texture — slightly rough edges, slight deliberate misalignment in print registration (not perfectly crisp digital lines), ink bleed imperfections. The overall feel is an authentic vintage athletic screen print patch from the 1990s. Background: TRANSPARENT."*

### [NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS]
> *"No mockup, no shirt shown, isolated graphic only, transparent background. NO PROPS — the floating posture and empty expression are the entire joke; no sunglasses, no hats, no objects, no accessories. Avoid photorealism, realistic anatomy, realistic fur, over-detailed illustration, thin outlines, clean digital vector lines, watercolor texture, smooth gradients, glossy rendering, airbrushing, or 3D shading. STRICTLY AVOID 3D text, 3D extrusion, drop shadows on text, isometric lettering, cursive/serif fonts, decorative typography, overly melting or noodly anatomy, complex fingers/toes, extra limbs, mechanical props, text-heavy props, or solid background colors. The design must read as one cohesive flat graphic — not a scene, not a photograph, not a detailed illustration."*
